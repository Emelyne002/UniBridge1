UniBridge

UniBridge is a cross-chain bridge that allows university students, lecturers, and administrators to communicate without delay. It connects all people around the university through calls, messages, and emails.

Why UniBridge?

Universities are large communities where communication often breaks down. Students struggle to reach lecturers, staff face delays in approvals, and important information gets lost in scattered channels. UniBridge solves this by providing a unified platform where every member of the university can connect instantly.

  • Eliminate delays in lecturer-student communication
  • Centralize scattered university announcements
  • Streamline administrative approval processes
  • Reduce lost information across multiple channels

Direct Calls

Connect with lecturers and staff through instant voice calls. No more waiting days for office hours.

Instant Messaging

Send quick messages to peers, teachers, and admin. Stay connected in real-time with your university community.

Email System

Professional email communication for formal requests, applications, and official documentation.

How It Works

UniBridge connects everyone in the university ecosystem. Whether you're a student needing help with coursework, a lecturer wanting to share announcements, or an administrator managing schedules, our platform bridges the gap.

  1. Sign up with your verified university email address
  2. Find the lecturers, staff, or students you need to reach
  3. Choose your communication method: call, message, or email
  4. Stay connected and collaborate in real-time
